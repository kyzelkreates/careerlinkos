# CareerLink OS™ — Safety & Disclaimer Module

**CareerLink OS™ — Job Search Compliance Dashboard + Jobseeker Activity PWA**
*Powered by 4P3X Intelligent AI — Created by Kyzel Kreates*

---

## Platform Disclaimer

CareerLink OS™ supports job-search tracking, evidence organisation, and employment support workflows.
It does not replace official guidance, legal advice, benefits advice, medical advice, or human decision-making.

---

## AI Safety Rules

**AI Disclaimer (required in all AI panels):**
> "CareerLink AI provides guidance, organisation support, and dashboard help only. It does not replace
> official guidance, legal advice, medical advice, benefits advice, or human coach/caseworker review."

### AI Modes
| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| OFF | AI features disabled |
| LOCAL / Rule-based | Offline advisory. No API key required. |
| API-READY | External provider connected. Falls back to local if no key set. |

### Human Override Requirement
Coaches and caseworkers must be able to review, override, correct, or annotate any AI-generated
risk levels, suggestions, or insights before action is taken.

---

## Reports Disclaimer

**Required on all generated reports:**
> "This report is a support and evidence summary. It does not guarantee eligibility, compliance,
> benefit entitlement, employment outcome, or government acceptance."

---

## Data Safety

- All data is stored locally in the browser (localStorage)
- No jobseeker personal data is sent to external services unless an AI provider API key is explicitly configured
- Demo records are marked `isDemo: true` and can be removed at any time via Settings → Demo Mode
- Coaches should follow their organisation's data protection and safeguarding policies

---

## Prohibited Claims

The app must NOT claim or imply:
- Guaranteed employment
- Guaranteed job-search compliance
- Guaranteed DWP/government acceptance
- Guaranteed benefits entitlement
- Automatic eligibility decisions
- Legal advice
- Medical advice
- Mental health diagnosis
- Official government decision-making
- AI replacement of human coach/caseworker judgment
